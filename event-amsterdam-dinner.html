Het Amsterdam Dinner

The Dolls Initiative was invited by the organizing foundation to attend Het Amsterdam Dinner, one of the most prestigious fundraising galas in the Netherlands dedicated to fighting HIV and AIDS stigma. Ten Dolls attended as special guests, joining hundreds of other supporters. Together, we helped raise an incredible €1.4 million to support HIV prevention, care, and the fight against stigma. Our presence celebrated the power of community, visibility, and solidarity in the ongoing battle against HIV.
